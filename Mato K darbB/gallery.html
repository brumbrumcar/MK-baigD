Rower design

A modern and stylish bean bag, slightly wider than the standard Seat model bean bag. Comfortable backrest and headrest support, along with a wide seating area, ensure an even more enjoyable and exceptionally comfortable rest. The Seat 100 is adapted to various needs: convenient for working or playing computer games, reading books, enjoying summer evenings on the terrace, or watching movies with your loved ones. The design of the Seat 100 bean bag is perfect for those who want to create a cozy, modern, and contemporary interior or exterior.

 

Lounge design

A stylish and comfortable bean bag, slightly wider than the standard Lounge model bean bag. The furniture features an exceptionally wide backrest and a raised seating area, so you can be sure to quickly forget about any leg fatigue. The design of the Lounge 100 bean bag is tailored for larger spaces: country house terraces, relaxation rooms, or spacious living rooms. This furniture will ensure pleasant relaxation for multiple individuals at the same time.

 

Grand design

This is a completely new experience of relaxation, seating, and lounging, inducing a light addiction to comfort, and you might even find yourself not wanting to sit on any other sofas or armchairs. The Grand 135 is designed for super comfortable lounging for taller individuals or even multiple people at once. Inside the bean bag, high-quality elastic foam is used, which does not sag, and the furniture returns to its original shape, allowing you to enjoy the weightlessness, falling into the bean bag experience multiple times. All bean bags come with an inner and outer cover, so you can easily change, wash, and re-dress your bean bag.
